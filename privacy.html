vNotes — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-05

vNotes ("the extension") lets you highlight, annotate, capture, and organize content from the web, and optionally sync it across your devices. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

1. What we collect

  • Content you create: highlights, notes, screenshots, screen recordings, and files you upload, plus the page URL and title they are associated with.
  • Account information (only if you sign in): your Google account email, display name, and profile photo, obtained through Google Sign-In.
  • We do not collect browsing history, keystrokes, passwords, or analytics/tracking data. There are no third-party ad or tracking SDKs.

2. How your data is stored

  • Without signing in: everything stays locally on your device (browser storage). Nothing leaves your computer.
  • If you sign in: your content is synced to a private, per-user area of our Google Firebase (Firestore + Cloud Storage) backend so you can access it on other devices. Each user can only access their own data.
  • Sharing: if you explicitly send a share request and the other person approves it, the two of you can view each other's shared highlights, screenshots, files, and recordings. Sharing never happens automatically and can be limited to people you connect with.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the extension's features — storing, displaying, syncing, and (where you choose) sharing your own content. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or any third party for marketing.

4. Permissions

  • identity — Google Sign-In for optional cloud sync.
  • storage, unlimitedStorage — save your highlights/notes/files locally and manage sync.
  • activeTab, tabs, scripting, host access — to display highlights and the toolbar on the pages you choose to annotate.
  • contextMenus — right-click highlight shortcuts.

5. Data retention & deletion

You can delete any item at any time from within the extension. Deleting an item removes it from your device and, if synced, from the cloud. To remove all cloud data, delete your items or contact us to request account-data deletion.

6. Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage) to provide sign-in and sync. Their handling of data is governed by the Google/Firebase Privacy Policy.

7. Children

vNotes is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
